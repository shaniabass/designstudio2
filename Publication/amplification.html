Material To be Amplified: Toxic Masculinity's Effect On Mental Health In Males


The promotion of toxic masiculinity in society has been prevalent for years and has led to many issues such as bullying and the fear of coming out as gay. However, a major issue is the idea that "Boys Don't Cry", resulting in many men and boys being afraid to express emotions that are not seen as "macho" or "manly", such as fear, anxiety or depression.

As a result, many men who do suffer from depression or suicidal thoughts are afraid to seek help. Because of this, even though more women suffer from
suicidal depression overall, an overwhelming majority of men actually go through with taking their own lives as their mental health issues often go completely
unnoticed or are not taken seriously. A staggering 79% of all US suicides result in the deaths of males.

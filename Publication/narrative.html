Event: Preparing Food


I documented myself preparing breakfast, lunch and dinner in 3 different ways: showing the tools but not the food
or the actions, showing the food at different stages but not the tools or the actions, and showing the actions but
not the food.




Reflection: It was recommended in critique that I choose less 'cliche' background music as the current track is quite
predictable. It was also suggested that I could chop and mix up the three videos into one to avoid them feeling like
instructional cooking videos.
